# Blueprint / Technical Drawing

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

White-line technical drawings on deep blue cyanotype paper — precise architectural blueprints, mechanical schematics, and engineering drawings with dimension lines, section markers, and notation. The visual language of engineering and architecture before CAD, where every line was drawn by hand with ruling pens and every measurement was annotated precisely. The result is simultaneously technical and aesthetically beautiful.

**Best for:** Desktop wallpapers · Social media posts · Art prints · Product concept art · Presentation backgrounds · T-shirt designs · Educational content

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices
- **White on blue is the signature**: "White lines on deep blue cyanotype paper" — this is what makes it a blueprint vs. a technical drawing.
- **Dimension lines and labels**: Engineering annotations (measurements, section markers, component labels) authenticate the technical feel.
- **Title block**: Every professional blueprint has a title block in the lower right with drawing name, number, and revision.
- **Fold crease marks**: "Folding crease marks on the paper" adds the physical feel of a real blueprint pulled from a drawer.
