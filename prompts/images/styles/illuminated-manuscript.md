# Illuminated Manuscript

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

Medieval hand-lettered pages with gold leaf, elaborate decorated initials, intricate vine and acanthus leaf borders, and miniature painted scenes in the margins. Inspired by the Book of Kells, Les Très Riches Heures, and the Lindisfarne Gospels.
