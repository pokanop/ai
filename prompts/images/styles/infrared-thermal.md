# Infrared Thermal Photography

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

False-color heat-map aesthetic where hot subjects glow in yellows, reds, and whites against cool blue and purple backgrounds. The distinctive look of FLIR thermal imaging cameras — functional, scientific, and visually striking.

![Infrared Thermal Example](../../../assets/placeholder-infrared.jpeg)

> **Sample prompt used to generate the above image (Nano Banana 2):**
> ```text
> Infrared thermal camera photograph of a large cat (tiger) prowling through a dense forest
> at night, 16:9 landscape format. The tiger's body glows intensely in bright yellows and
> oranges — hottest at the face, ears, and paw pads — with its core radiating white-hot
> thermal energy. The tail and extremities cool to warm reds and magentas. The surrounding
> foliage is rendered in cool blues and deep purples, with slightly warmer teal patches where
> sunlight heated the leaves earlier. The ground shows blue-purple with faint warm footprints
> trailing behind the tiger. False-color thermal palette with a temperature scale bar along
> the right edge. Slight pixelation characteristic of thermal sensor resolution.
> ```

**ChatGPT**
```text
Create an infrared thermal camera photograph of [SUBJECT] in a [ENVIRONMENT]. The subject's warmest areas glow in bright yellows and whites, transitioning through oranges and reds in cooler zones. The surrounding environment is rendered in cool blues and deep purples. Use the standard false-color thermal palette (FLIR Ironbow or similar). Include a temperature scale bar along the right edge. Apply slight pixelation characteristic of thermal sensor resolution. The contrast between the hot subject and cold environment should be dramatic and immediately readable.
```
