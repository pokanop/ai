# Pixar / 3D Animation

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

Expressive 3D animated characters with large emotive eyes, soft subsurface-scattered skin, and whimsical, colorful environments. The quintessential family-film aesthetic.

![Pixar Example](../../../assets/placeholder-pixar.jpeg)

> **Sample prompt used to generate the above image (Nano Banana 2):**
> ```text
> 3D animated feature film still of a grandmother and her granddaughter baking cookies
> together in a warm, cluttered kitchen, Pixar animation style, 16:9 landscape format. The
> grandmother has round spectacles and flour on her nose; the little girl stands on a stool,
> tongue poking out in concentration. Large expressive eyes with detailed iris reflections on
> both characters. Warm amber key light from the oven with cool blue window fill. Cookies,
> dough, and rolling pins scattered across the countertop.
> ```

**ChatGPT**
```text
Create a 3D animated movie still of [SUBJECT] in the signature style of a Pixar feature film. The character has large, expressive eyes with detailed iris reflections, soft rubbery skin with subsurface scattering, and exaggerated but appealing proportions. They are standing in a whimsical, colorful [ENVIRONMENT] illuminated by warm bounce lighting with a soft amber key light and cool blue fill. The composition should feel like a promotional poster frame.
```
