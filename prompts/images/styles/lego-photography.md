# Lego Photography

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

Macro photography of Lego minifigures and brick-built environments, capturing the charm of plastic textures, injection mold seams, and classic Lego colors.

![Lego Example](../../../assets/placeholder-lego.jpeg)

> **Sample prompt used to generate the above image (Nano Banana 2):**
> ```text
> Macro photograph of a Lego astronaut minifigure planting a tiny Lego flag on a miniature Lego Mars base, 16:9 landscape format. A small Lego rover with translucent orange wheels parked nearby. Glossy ABS plastic texture with visible injection mold seam lines on every brick. Classic Lego colors — white spacesuit, red planet surface, blue flag. Cinematic side-lighting highlighting the plastic sheen, tilt-shift macro lens with shallow depth of field.
> ```

**ChatGPT**
```text
Create a macro photograph of a Lego minifigure depicting [SUBJECT], placed inside a miniature environment built entirely from Lego bricks representing [ENVIRONMENT]. Light the scene cinematically to highlight the glossy plastic sheen and visible injection mold seam lines on each brick. Use a macro lens with a shallow depth of field so the foreground figure is tack-sharp while the brick-built background falls gently out of focus. Classic bright Lego primary colors throughout.
```
