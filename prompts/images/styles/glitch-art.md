# Glitch Art

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

Digital corruption aesthetics — scan lines, pixel sorting, data-moshing, chromatic aberration, and RGB channel displacement. The visual language of broken technology embraced as art. Glitch art finds beauty in the accidental — corrupted JPEG artifacts, displaced pixel rows, color channel splits, and the eerie patterns that emerge when digital systems fail. The result feels simultaneously broken and beautiful, unsettling and hypnotic.

**Best for:** Social media posts · Album covers · Editorial illustrations · Profile pictures · Phone wallpapers · Music visualizers · Cyberpunk themed content

> Glitch art portrait of a woman looking directly at camera, 4:5 vertical format. The image appears corrupted — horizontal scan lines distort sections of the face, RGB color channels split apart with red shifted left and cyan shifted right, pixel-sorted vertical streaks drag portions of the hair downward into data ribbons. JPEG artifact blocks appear in the corrupted zones. The uncorrupted areas are crisp and photorealistic, creating contrast with the broken sections. Neon cyan and magenta dominate the color palette against a dark background. The overall effect is beautiful and unsettling.
> ```

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

- **Name specific glitch types**: "Pixel sorting," "data-moshing," "RGB channel displacement," "JPEG artifact blocks" — each produces a distinct effect. Combine 2-3 for the best results.
- **Contrast matters**: The most compelling glitch art has areas of clarity alongside areas of corruption. "Uncorrupted areas remain crisp" creates this duality.
- **Neon palette on dark**: The classic glitch art palette is neon cyan, magenta, and hot pink against dark backgrounds. The neon pops through the corruption.
- **VHS vs. digital**: "VHS tracking error" produces horizontal band distortion. "JPEG corruption" produces blocky artifacts. "Data-moshing" produces motion smears. Choose based on the mood.
- **Common pitfalls**: Avoid "slightly glitched" — go bold or the effect barely registers. Don't overload every type of glitch — choose 2-3 and commit.
- **Pairs well with:** [Cyberpunk Noir](cyberpunk-noir.md) (similar neon-on-dark palette), [Vaporwave / Synthwave Retrowave](vaporwave-synthwave.md) (shares the digital nostalgia aesthetic)
