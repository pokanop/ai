# Made of Yarn / Amigurumi

[← Back to Image Prompts](../README.md)

Cozy, tactile scenes where everything — characters and environments — is crafted from knitted and crocheted yarn with visible stitches, fuzz, and warm lighting.

![Yarn Example](../../../assets/placeholder-yarn.jpeg)

> **Sample prompt used to generate the above image (Nano Banana 2):**
> ```text
> Macro photograph of a tiny crocheted baby dragon sleeping on top of a stack of miniature knitted books, 16:9 landscape format. The dragon is made from emerald-green and gold yarn with individually visible stitches and tiny fuzzy fibers catching the light along its wings. One nostril releases a single wisp of crocheted orange "flame" yarn. The books have different colored yarn spines. Everything sits on a knitted patchwork quilt. Soft warm directional lighting from a miniature yarn fireplace glowing in the background.
> ```

**ChatGPT**
```text
Create a macro photograph of an adorable amigurumi crocheted doll of [SUBJECT], made entirely from soft yarn. Every stitch should be individually visible, with tiny fuzzy fibers catching the light along the edges. The doll sits in a miniature [ENVIRONMENT] where every element — furniture, plants, ground — is also knitted or crocheted from yarn. Light the scene with soft, warm directional light that emphasizes the cozy textile texture.
```
